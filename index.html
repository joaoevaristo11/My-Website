About Me

I'm João Evaristo, a second-year university student pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering - Networks and Telecommunications at ISEL. Since I was young, I have been passionate about video games, which led me to spend a lot of time on computers and, ultimately, to choose this career path. My technical background includes software development (Java, Python, C), database management (SQL), and networking (VLANs, Routing, Switching). I also have hands-on experience in network analysis and troubleshooting, using tools like Wireshark, Packet Tracer, and GNS3.


Apart from technology, I have always been a sports enthusiast, having practiced football, swimming, gym workouts, calisthenics, and running. I am constantly striving to improve myself and feel like I am never quite where I should be, as if something is always missing. My goal is to work for a major company in my field and, one day, build my own business.
